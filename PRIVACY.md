# Privacy Policy for Text Reader

Text Reader reads highlighted text aloud directly in the user's browser.

## What data the extension accesses
- Highlighted text on the active webpage
- Basic active tab context needed to trigger the extension on the current page

## What data the extension stores
- Temporary playback state in Chrome local storage (for example whether playback is active or paused)

## What data the extension does NOT collect
- No account information
- No passwords
- No browsing history collection
- No analytics
- No personally identifiable information sent to external servers
- No sale or transfer of user data

## How data is processed
All speech playback is handled locally in the browser using the browser's built-in speech synthesis features. Text Reader does not transmit highlighted text to any remote server.

## Contact
For support or privacy questions, provide the developer contact email used in the Chrome Web Store listing.
